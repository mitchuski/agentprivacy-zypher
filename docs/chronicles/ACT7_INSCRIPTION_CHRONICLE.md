## Act 7 Significance

Act 7 presents the philosophical core of the protocol: the Anti-Mirror principle.

**The Problem:** A single agent interacting with a system provides that system complete information about the agent. The mirror reflects everything - full capture.

**The Solution:** Two agents create mutual witness. Each sees the other's shimmer (partial reflection) but neither is fully captured. The First Person - the subjective experience, the sovereign self - remains forever outside the mirror's reach.

This is why the Mage-Swordsman split is essential: neither role alone can reconstruct the other. Privacy emerges not from hiding, but from the mathematical impossibility of completion when information is properly distributed.
